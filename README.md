### PROJECT: TerminBuddy
**Networking platform for discovering, publishing, and organizing sports sessions with a focus on community building.**

---

### ABOUT THE PROJECT
**TerminBuddy** is a software solution designed to digitally transform the process of scheduling and joining sports activities. The core focus of the platform is connecting individuals through a shared passion for sports, social interaction, and team play.

Moving away from traditional, fragmented communication methods, TerminBuddy offers a centralized system for organization, real-time communication, and social networking. Whether you are an introvert looking for a quiet game with few people or an extrovert seeking a large tournament, the platform makes it effortless to find the right match and company.

---

### KEY FEATURES & FUNCTIONALITY
TerminBuddy is designed to be more than just a scheduling tool; it is a comprehensive sports ecosystem:

* **Dynamic Publishing & Booking:** Users can easily create their own sports sessions via a streamlined form or browse existing ones. Joining a session can be instant or subject to approval by the creator.
* **Advanced Filtering:** Find exactly what you're looking for by filtering sessions based on sport type, location, time, and skill level.
* **Real-time Communication:** Once a user joins or expresses interest in a session, they can participate in dedicated chats with other participants to coordinate details.
* **Tournament Management:** A dedicated module for creating and managing competitive tournaments.
* **Team Registrations:** Facilitates the registration of entire teams for tournaments, tracking brackets and team rosters.
* **Social Networking:** Focus on building a local sports community, making it easy to meet new people and find consistent teammates.

---

### TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION
The project is built using a modern, industry-standard technology stack to ensure performance, security, and scalability:

* **Next.js & React:** Powering a robust and fast frontend with the latest App Router architecture.
* **Supabase (SQL):** Serving as the backend infrastructure, providing a powerful PostgreSQL database, real-time subscriptions, and secure authentication.
* **Tailwind CSS:** Ensuring a modern, mobile-first responsive design across all devices.
* **Vercel Deployment:** Utilized for continuous integration and high-availability hosting.
